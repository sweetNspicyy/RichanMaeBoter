About Ark-Asia

To view the full image, click on any photo.

ArkAsia Constructors and Developers, Corp. is a construction and engineering company that specializes in building commercial establishments, designing structural plans, and managing full project developments. They handle various stages of construction from planning and drafting blueprints to supervising onsite progress and ensuring that every structure meets safety and quality standards. The company works closely with architects, civil engineers, electrical engineers, and project managers, making it a collaborative environment where different fields come together to complete a project. Their operations are well-organized and professional, which allowed us to observe how real engineering projects are planned, monitored, and executed.

ArkAsia Constructors and Developers, Corp. gave us a meaningful immersion experience by allowing us to explore real work related to Civil Engineering and Architecture. They guided us through reading blueprints, organizing project documents, and observing how construction plans are prepared and executed. The trainers also taught us how to use AutoCAD and SketchUp, letting us experience how engineers design structures. Through their support and hands-on activities, we gained a clearer understanding of the field and appreciated the effort, precision, and teamwork required in the engineering profession.

Day 1

Today’s immersion taught me how important it is to understand the real responsibilities of engineers and architectural designers. I realized that working in this field requires discipline, accuracy, and strong communication skills. Observing their teamwork made me appreciate how essential CLARITY and COORDINATION are in ensuring the progress of a project. This experience made me more grateful because we were given the opportunity to listen to actual reporting from different engineers. Overall, the day helped me grow by showing me the level of dedication and professionalism needed in the engineering industry.

Key Learnings

Understanding the Real Work in Engineering and Architectural World

My immersion helped me see how engineering works beyond textbooks. I learned how blueprints, floor plans, site visits, and materials all connect in actual projects. Seeing how engineers communicate, plan, and solve problems made me realize how detailed and challenging the engineering field really is. It also showed me the importance of accuracy, teamwork, and responsibility in every construction project.

Hands-On Skills

Throughout the immersion, I gained practical experience using AutoCAD and SketchUp, organizing blueprints, and checking project documents. These tasks helped me understand the technical side of Civil Engineering and Architecture. Even though the work was sometimes difficult, especially with measurements and specifications, it taught me patience and improved my confidence in using engineering tools and software.

Inspiration and Guidance

ArkAsia Constructors and Developers, Corp. created a very supportive learning environment. The engineers and trainers were approachable, patient, and willing to guide us at every step. Their dedication and professionalism inspired me to pursue engineering in college. Their advice especially about the challenges and expectations in the field helped me become more prepared and motivated for my future career.
